All about My Dog Chunks

My dog Chunks is a 12 year old chocolate lab. He has been like a child for my husband and I. We got him after being married for just a year. He is getting old a grey. One of the funniest things he has ever said is, Bark bark, barkey bark He is a real hoot if you ever get the chance to meet him.

Chunk's Routine

Chunks has a pretty set routine after all these years and gets pretty annoyed when things dont go accordingly. Here is his daily schedule:

  1. Wakes up
  2. Goes outside to use the bathroom
  3. Comes in to eat breakfast
  4. Naps
  5. Wants back out to bark at birds
  6. Naps and plays until dinner
  7. Back to bed

All the places I have lived:

  • Mayflower, AR
  • Colorado Springs, CO
  • Benton, AR

Gage was in the military when we first got Chunks, who at the time had been living in Mayflower. He moved several times, thankfully he is a good car rider.
